How It Works

Three steps. Hundreds of applications.

No scripts. No coding. Just download, configure, and let AutoApply do the rest.

1

Set Your Preferences

Pick your job titles, locations, experience level, and salary range. The onboarding wizard handles everything in under 5 minutes.

2

Click Start

One button. AutoApply opens LinkedIn, navigates to Easy Apply jobs, fills out every form, and submits applications on your behalf.

3

Track Your Progress

Your dashboard updates in real-time. See every application, success rate, and review any jobs that were skipped.

Features

Built for the modern job hunt.

Everything you need to scale your job search without the manual grind.

Smart Form Filling

Handles dropdowns, radio buttons, text fields, and file uploads automatically. No form is too complex.

Never Double Apply

Every application is tracked in a local database. Run it daily without worry — it remembers where you left off.

Rate Limit Protection

Automatically pauses before hitting LinkedIn's daily cap. Random delays mimic human behavior. Resume tomorrow.

Encrypted & Local

Your credentials never leave your machine. AES-256 encryption at rest. No cloud, no servers, no third parties.

Easy Apply Only

Filters out external application links automatically. Only targets jobs where it can apply directly through LinkedIn.

AI-Ready Logs

Structured JSON logs for every session. Feed them to ChatGPT or Claude for application analysis and optimization.

Dashboard

Your command center.

Track every application. See what worked. Review what didn't.
